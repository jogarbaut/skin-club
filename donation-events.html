Donation Events

How Donation Events Work

Stop by during the designated times at the listed locations to donate your qualifying sunscreen products for us to distribute throughout our community! Your donations allow us to spread our message and save lives!

Requirements

Sunscreen must be SPF >30, Water-Proof, Broad Spectrum, and preferably non-spray.
